If a member is affected by the public service pensions remedy (McCloud), you can use this service to find out when their Remediable Service Statement should arrive.

An RSS shows the member's pension benefits under:

  • the 2015 NHS Pension Scheme
  • their legacy NHS Pension Scheme – which is the 1998 or 2008 Scheme, depending on which one they were a member of before 1 April 2022

Members can then compare the benefits and decide what they'd like to take.

You can read more about who is affected by McCloud (opens in a new tab).

Before you start

We'll ask you for the member's:

  • NHS Pensions membership number or National Insurance number
  • date of birth

If you're using this service to find out the delivery date for someone who has died, you will need to know their information.
