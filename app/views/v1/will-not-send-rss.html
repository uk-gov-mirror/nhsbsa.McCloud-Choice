We'll not send a remediable service statement (RSS) to this member

The member is a pension credit member and will not make a choice about their benefits.

What is a pension credit member?

This is a former partner of an NHS Pension Scheme member. They then became a member themselves through a pension sharing order due to a divorce or dissolution.

What happens next

We'll give pension credit members the best pension benefits. This means they may get an uplift in their benefits.

It does not matter what Scheme their former partner chooses to take their own benefits from if they're affected by McCloud.

We will send the member more information about this after their former partner has made their choice.
