This member is not due a Remediable Service Statement (RSS) yet

We'll not send this member's RSS yet because their pension payments have not started.

Some members may need their RSS sooner if they meet certain criteria. You can read more about this in our When you'll make your choice (opens in new tab) page.

If the member's payments have started

If the member's payments have started recently, it's possible we do not have a delivery date for their RSS yet.

You can check again another day or contact us using our Contact NHS Pensions (opens in new tab) service.

If the member is partially retired and has 1995, 2008 and 2015 Scheme membership

Some partially retired members impacted by the McCloud remedy will not make a choice about their benefits yet. This is because the benefits they currently receive do not include the McCloud remedy impacted ones.

This can happen if the member:

  • has 1995 Scheme membership
  • left the Scheme and re-joined the 2008 Scheme - meaning they were a member of this Scheme during the remedy period
  • has partially retired and taken benefits from the 1995 Scheme only

Their McCloud remedy choice is between 2008 and 2015 Scheme benefits. When the member starts receiving their full pension, we'll send them an RSS.
